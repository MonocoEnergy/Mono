# Monoco is a project that considered by a group of young engineers to produce the clean energy. And they started working with ANNA FLO Company on the project. The ANNA FLO Company started operation in Turkey "2014". As we know renewable energy is called as clean energy, which comes from natural sources or processes that are constantly replenished. Our project use Nano technology for producing this clean energy which is more effective and cheaper. the goal of our project is to extend clean energy as a part of daily energy producer, to decentralized and democratize the consumption, transmission and production of solar energy. We're trying to launch the largest decentralized solar power plant.
# Monoco has a Token (Mono) On the Tron Network.
